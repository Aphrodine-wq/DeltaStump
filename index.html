Disaster Relief

Oxford, MS FEMA & Insurance Stump Removal Guide.

If you experienced property damage during Winter Storm Fern in Lafayette County, you may be eligible for financial relief to cover stump grinding and removal costs.

01
Check Your Homeowner's Policy First If a storm-damaged tree fell on a covered structure (like your house, fence, or driveway) or blocked a primary access route, your homeowner's insurance will often cover the cost of removing the tree and grinding the stump.
02
Understanding FEMA Assistance in MS If Lafayette County is declared a federal disaster area and you are uninsured or underinsured, FEMA's Individuals and Households Program (IHP) may provide funds. FEMA typically only covers stump removal if the uprooted stump poses an immediate threat to life, public health, or safety.
03
Document Everything Before We Grind Take clear photos of the uprooted tree and the remaining stump before any work begins. Do not pay for grinding until you have documented the hazard. We will provide a detailed invoice specifically outlining "stump grinding and hazard removal" for your claims adjuster.
