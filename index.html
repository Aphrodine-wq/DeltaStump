What we do

Stump grinding. Land clearing. Cleanup.

Stump Grinding

Got one stump or twenty — we grind them down below grade and fill the hole back in with chips. Residential, commercial, farm — doesn't matter.

Land Clearing

Prepping a lot for construction or just cleaning up a neglected field? We clear out stumps and surface roots.

Cleanup

We spread the chips as mulch or haul everything off. You pick. Either way, the yard is clean when we leave.
