Started in 2008

Delta Stump got started because people kept asking if anyone could come get rid of stumps in their yards. Turns out, not many outfits had the right equipment to do it properly.

So we got a grinder, put the word out, and got to work. That was over 15 years ago. We've been at it ever since.

What we're about

We're not a big landscaping company. We don't do tree trimming, lawn care, or any of that. We grind stumps and clear land. That's our thing, and we've gotten pretty good at it over the years.

We show up when we say we will, we charge a fair price, and we don't leave a mess behind. Most of our work comes from repeat customers and referrals. We take that as a good sign.

Where we work

We're based in Oxford and serve Oxford, MS and the surrounding areas. If you're not sure whether we come out your way, just call and ask. We'll tell you straight.
