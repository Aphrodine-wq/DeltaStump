Who we are

Delta Stump is a newer name in the area, but the guys running the equipment are regulars when it comes to yard work and tree service in Oxford.

We noticed an ongoing issue for reliable, straightforward stump grinding. Too many large outfits didn't want the small jobs, and too many folks couldn't get a call back. So we stepped in to fill that gap with dedicated equipment and a commitment to communication.

What we do (and don't do)

We're not a big landscaping company. We don't do full tree removals, lawn care, or land clearing. We strictly grind stumps and remove surface roots. That's our specialty.

We believe in doing one thing right. We show up when we say we will, we charge a fair flat rate, and we clean up before we leave. We know that if we treat people right, the work keeps coming.

Where we work

We're based in Oxford and serve Oxford, MS and the surrounding areas. If you're not sure whether we come out your way, just call and ask. We'll tell you straight.
