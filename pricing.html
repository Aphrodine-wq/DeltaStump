Transparent Pricing

We give you a firm number before we start. Here's how we charge.

Stump Grinding

Pricing is based on the size (diameter) of the stump. Single stumps typically run $75–$200. Multiple stumps get a better rate. A typical job runs $200–$600. We give you a flat price before we start — no hourly guesswork.

Surface Root Removal

For surface roots trailing across the yard, we come out and look at the property first. Pricing depends on the overall length of the roots, density, and access. We give you a flat bid — not an hourly estimate. Call with the address and rough length; we'll tell you what it'll cost.

Cleanup

Spreading the chips in place is included. If you want every last chip hauled off the property, that's an add-on — we'll factor it into your estimate when you call.
